## Full narration (plain text)

Welcome to HIRA. Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment — score risk on a 5×5 matrix and apply the hierarchy of controls.

In this short tutorial, we'll walk through how to use the app.

First, register your organization. The first person to sign up becomes the administrator.

Enter your details and a password, then create the organization.

Your risk dashboard. Live KPIs: assessments, risk bands and control status.

Create a risk assessment. Define activities, identify hazards (GEMS taxonomy), then score probability × severity.

Assessment repository. Every completed assessment, filterable by site, name and location.

Action Tracker. Track control measures through to closure with due dates and owners.

Bulk import. Import many hazards at once from a CSV template.

HIRA — assess, control, track.. Start by registering your organization.

That's a quick tour of HIRA. Thanks for watching — get started by registering your organization.
